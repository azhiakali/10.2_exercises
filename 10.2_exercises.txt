Quiz
What is the difference between var and let?
var can be reassigned and reclared. let can be reassigned but not redeclared.

What is the difference between var and const?
const can't be reassigned or redeclared.

What is the difference between let and const?
let can be reassigned, const can not.

What is hoisting?
Variables are hoisting to the top of the scope. With var, you can access it before it is used.
